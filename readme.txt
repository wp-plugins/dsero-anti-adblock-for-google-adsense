== Installation ==

1. Unzip the file you downloaded into your /wp-content/plugins directory
1. From the WordPress admin section, click on **Plugins**.
1. Activate the dSero plugin.
1. In the settings section choose **dSero Anti AdBlock**.
1. Recover your site's lost revenue by submitting your email.

If you encounter any problems during installation, please email us at support@dsero.com.

In order to **uninstall** the dSero Anti AdBlock plugin:

1. From the WordPress admin section, click on **Plugins**.
1. Next to the dSero Anti AdBlock plugin, click **Deactivate** followed by **Delete**.
1. After the plugin has been deleted, your revenue recovery will be deactivated.
